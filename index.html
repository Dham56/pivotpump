⚙️ Pumps

▾

Each pump's max capacity is the gallons/hour it injects at 100%. The setting is a percentage of that, so injection rate = max capacity × setting% ÷ 100. Edit a pump here and every pivot using it updates. Assign a pump to a pivot in the pivot's Edit panel.

💾 Backup / Restore

▾

Your pumps & pivots are saved only in this browser — clear it or switch phones and they're gone. Tap Copy setup, then paste the text into a note or email it to yourself. To restore (or move to a new phone), paste that text back in and tap Restore.

🧾 Activity log

▾

A running record of changes you make in the app — pump & pivot edits, passes, calibrations, restores — so you can look back and figure out what happened. Syncs to your other devices when you're signed in to Cloud sync.
